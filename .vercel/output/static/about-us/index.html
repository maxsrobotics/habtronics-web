Habtronics is a student-led organization that aims to facilitate open access to upper air environments to pioneer education and research opportunities. We are dedicated to providing high-altitude balloon tracking and recovery services to students and educators around the world. Our mission is to inspire the next generation of scientists, engineers, and explorers by providing hands-on experience in the field of high-altitude ballooning.
